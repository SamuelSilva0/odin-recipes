HABIB'S OPEN SFIHA

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 kg of wheat flour
  • 30g dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup (tea) refined sugar (125 g)
  • 125 ml soybean oil
  • 500 to 700ml of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon refined salt
  • cornmeal to sprinkle

STUFFING (CHEESE):

  • 700g mashed white cheese
  • 1 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 cup chopped chives
  • 6 tablespoons sour cream
  • 1 tsp baking powder (used to give consistency and standardize the filling)
  • salt to taste

MEAT STUFFING:

  • 700g of ground beef
  • 5 chopped tomatoes
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup mint (tea)
  • juice of 2 lemons
  • Salt and pepper to taste

METHOD OF PREPARATION

FILLING:

  1. Combine all ingredients for the chosen filling
  2. Mix well, kneading with your hands and book.

PASTA:

  1. Mix 500 ml of water (reserve the rest) with the yeast and sugar and let stand for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the flour (separate a small part), oil and salt.
  3. Mix with a wooden spoon.
  4. Then pour and knead the dough on a floured surface.
  5. Add the rest of the flour alternating with the water little by little until the dough looks homogeneous and comes off the hands.
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes.
  7. After resting, divide the dough into 2 equal parts and make a thick roll with each of them.
  8. Measure three fingers and cut all the dough into equal pieces.
  9. Next, make balls using the cornmeal to sprinkle and shape the dough. Using your fingers, flatten each ball making high sides (like a nest).
  10. Accommodate the discs in the form sprinkled with cornmeal and let it rest for 15 minutes.
  11. Place a small portion of stuffing on each sfiha disc. Spread the stuffing evenly and bake in a preheated oven at 180ยบ.
  12. Serve hot or cold.
